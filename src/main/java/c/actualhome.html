Welcome Admin

Here you can perform various tasks in order to complete defect metrics sheet. Tasks are as described below

  • You can enter your defect metrics for the week, to do this you can click on "Defect Metrics" or click here
  • You can fill up defects validated in the current week, to do this you can click on "Defects Validated in Week" or click here
  • You can enter number of defects regressed as part of packet regression by clicking on "Packet regression" or click here
  • You can mail defect metrics sheet in order to do this click on "Mail To!" or click here
  • You can check whether all the team members are submitted or not, by clicking on "Status" or Click here
  • You can reset all the values of metrics sheet inorder to do this click on "reset" or click here
